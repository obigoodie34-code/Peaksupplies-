Refund & Return Policy
Effective: January 2025
1. Our Commitment

Peak Supplies conducts quality inspections before any item is shipped. We are committed to ensuring every client receives products that match their order description.

2. Eligibility for Returns

You may request a return if: the item arrives visibly damaged, is significantly different from what was ordered, or is non-functional on arrival. You must notify us within 48 hours of delivery with photos.

3. Non-Returnable Items

Items are non-returnable if: you ordered the wrong item or size, the item has been used or altered, or the complaint was raised more than 48 hours after delivery.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method. Replacements are dispatched within 3-5 business days of approval.

5. Shipping Costs

For items returned due to our error, Peak Supplies covers return shipping costs. For other returns, the client covers return shipping.

6. Contact Us

To initiate a return, WhatsApp us or use the dispute form on the website with your order reference number and photos. Our team will respond within 24 hours.
